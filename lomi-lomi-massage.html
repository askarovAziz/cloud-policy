Signature Lomi Lomi Massage in Iris Bay Tower, Business Bay

Lomi Lomi at Cloud9 Spa is a premium full-body massage inspired by flowing, rhythmic technique. Using smooth forearm strokes and continuous movements, this signature treatment is designed to ease tension, promote deep relaxation, and restore a calm sense of balance — perfect for unwinding in the heart of Iris Bay Tower, Business Bay, Dubai.

Our sessions are performed by professionally trained European therapists, including experienced Russian and Ukrainian specialists, known for refined technique and consistent pressure. Each treatment is personalized to your comfort level and preferences, delivered in a private and elegant spa environment.
