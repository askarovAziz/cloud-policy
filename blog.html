Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to help you plan your next visit to Cloud9 Spa

Do I need to book in advance?

We recommend booking ahead, especially for evening and weekend appointments. Same-day slots are available depending on therapist availability.

How early should I arrive?

Please arrive 10–15 minutes early to relax, complete any consultation notes, and enjoy the pre-session atmosphere.

What should I wear to my appointment?

Comfortable clothing is ideal. You will be provided with towels and privacy throughout your massage session.

Can I request a specific massage pressure?

Absolutely. Let your therapist know your preferred pressure level and any focus areas so we can tailor the experience.

Is parking available in Iris Bay Tower, Business Bay?

Yes, convenient parking is available nearby. Contact us if you need directions or assistance locating the best spot.
