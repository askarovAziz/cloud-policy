About Cloud9 Spa

Our story, mission, and values

Our Story

Cloud9 Spa was founded with a simple yet powerful idea: to create an oasis of calm in the heart of dynamic Dubai where every guest can experience authentic European spa artistry.

Founded in 2018 by a group of wellness enthusiasts from across Europe, Cloud9 Spa quickly became one of Dubai's most prestigious spa destinations. Our team blends the finest European resort traditions with modern wellness technologies.

We believe true beauty and health begin from within. That is why every treatment is not just a cosmetic procedure, but a holistic approach to the well-being of body, mind, and soul.

Located in Iris Bay Tower within the prestigious Business Bay district, our spa offers an escape from the city's rush without ever leaving town. Here, time slows down, stress fades, and you reconnect with yourself.
